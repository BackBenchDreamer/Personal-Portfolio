Safari-Sentinel

Safari Sentinel is an advanced AI-powered wildlife monitoring tool designed to identify and classify animals in real-time using the YOLOv8 model. It excels at recognizing a wide range of species with 98% accuracy, offering insights through bounding boxes and labels that include conservation status. Integrated with Google Colab, Safari Sentinel is user-friendly for both researchers and wildlife enthusiasts, contributing to innovative conservation efforts. Its focus on animal diversity makes it a versatile solution for tracking species in various habitats, aiming to revolutionize the way we monitor wildlife ecosystems.
